Bachelor topic title:
	Stock Price Prediction System (Web application)
	
Description of the problem:
	We need a unique connection to a database to manage the data required by all users while interacting with the application.

Advantages of the solution:
	- Data access is simple to handle;
	- Just a single connection to a certain database.
	
Disadvantages of the solution:
	- The presence of only one connection may have an impact on the application's overall performance;
	- The pattern may have an impact on unit testing procedures.
